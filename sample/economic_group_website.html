Our Journey

Three generations,
one unbroken thread.

From a single workshop to a multi-division manufacturing group — our story is one of craftsmanship, family discipline, and the relentless pursuit of quality across half a century.

I
1970s – 1990s
The Founding Vision
Our grandfather started with a small workshop and a single belief — that Indian hands could craft world-class footwear. He built the foundation: the machines, the methods, the discipline, and a reputation that would outlast him. Every pair that left that workshop carried his name and his integrity.
II
1990s – 2010s
Scaling the Legacy
The second generation expanded operations — growing production capacity, adding bag manufacturing as a second vertical, and securing the partnership as an authorised manufacturer for Bata India. They modernised the factory, tightened quality controls, and built a B2B network that reached retailers and distributors across India.
III
2010s – Present
Innovation & Continuity
The third generation brings 35+ years of business strategy and customer success experience to a manufacturing legacy built over five decades. We have launched Economic Solar, expanded our digital presence, and are exploring export markets — while maintaining every standard our grandfather established.
